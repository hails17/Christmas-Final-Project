  3. Make Oreo Truffles
    • 1 Block of Cream Cheese, 1 Pack of Oreos, 1 Bag of Melted Chocolate Chips (Double for larger serving size)
    • Using a mixing bowl, Crush up oreos until they are all crushed (the more granular, the better)
    • Put in block of Cream Cheese into bowl with crushed oreos. Mix cream cheese until all oreo pieces are woven into mixture
    • In seperate bowl, melt chocolate chips.
    • Roll oreo mixture into balls and then submerge in chocolate so that they are covered.
    • Place chocolate covered oreo ball into freezer and let freeze for 10 minutes
